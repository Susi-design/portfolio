About

I'am Susi Melinda

Hi, I'm Susi Melinda, a passionate and curious creative technologist who loves crafting meaningful digital experiences. With a background in UI/UX design, web development, and product prototyping, I enjoy working at the intersection of design and technology to build intuitive and visually compelling products. I have a strong eye for visual storytelling and specialize in creating product posters and promotional videos using animation, bringing ideas to life with motion and impact. I thrive on turning ideas into interactive designs—whether it's animating interfaces in Figma, developing responsive websites with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. I’m constantly learning, experimenting, and refining my craft, and I believe in clean code, clear communication, and thoughtful design. Whether it's a single screen or an entire product flow, I aim to make every pixel and function purposeful.
